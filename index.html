This is me

With coding as a passion and an eye for design, I’m always eager to jump on the next project knowing there is something to be learned or overcome. Subtly confident in everything I do, naturally curious, and always in search of a solution. As a Full-stack developer, I find being able to build something from scratch a beautiful skill.

Years of leadership roles have provided me with all the problem solving and time management skills necessary to make any project a success. This site, along with my Github, show my work as a developer; however, below you can find my resume and discover my efforts before I discovered my new passion.

Familiar with basics like HTML, CSS, and the MVC model, designs are really only limited by imagination. The use of frameworks, libraries & tools such as: React, Handlebars, Node, NPM & Webpack make it all the easier. Comfortable working with SQL databases, but let's face it.. there's no relation, document store is the way to go.
